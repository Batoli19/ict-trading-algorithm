WHAT EACH SETUP DOES
=====================

The bot looks for extremely specific patterns on the charts. Currently, it focuses heavily on two main setups.

1. CHOCH (Change of Character)
Imagine the price is steadily going down, making a staircase pattern downwards. Suddenly, it breaks the staircase and surges upwards past the last "step". This abrupt shift in momentum is called a Change of Character. It signals that the sellers are exhausted and buyers are stepping in. The bot waits for this specific break and then enters a trade in the new direction.

2. LSR (Liquidity Sweep Reversal / Turtle Soup)
The market is driven by liquidity (where people place their stop-loss orders). Often, the market will aggressively spike above an old high to "trap" retail traders into buying (and hit the stop-losses of early sellers). Immediately after this "sweep", the large institutions reverse the price back down. The LSR setup detects this exact fake-out and trades the reversal, riding along with the "smart money" instead of getting trapped.
